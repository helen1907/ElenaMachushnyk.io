A photographer is one who sees wider, feels deeper and subtly observes everything that adorns a person. After all, each of us looks at ourselves very critically. And in the reflection of the mirror can not see the whole truth about himself. It is important for the photographer to capture not only the present moment, emotions, but also to tell the story about the individual beauty of the personality, which is in its completely unique originality. In my opinion, the photographer’s happiness is when the client, seeing himself in the photo, becomes more confident in himself, maybe for the first time in his life he admires himself and falls in love even more. And how wonderful it is to realize that you touch the history of a person’s life and influence his fate. Great to be a photographer. :-)
